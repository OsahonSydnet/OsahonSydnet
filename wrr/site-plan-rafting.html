Rafting Site Plan

Xavier Rodriquez

Overview

Purpose

The primary purpose of establishing an online presence for our rafting business is to enhance our ability to attract and engage with customers by providing a comprehensive and accessible platform for all their rafting needs.

The website will serve several key functions:

  • Attract New Customers: Our website will be designed to capture the interest of adventure enthusiasts seeking thrilling rafting experiences. By showcasing the unique and exciting trips we offer, we aim to convert visitors into customers.
  • Provide Information: It will act as an informative hub, detailing everything from trip itineraries and pricing to safety protocols and what to bring. This clarity will help potential customers make informed decisions and reduce the number of common inquiries.
  • Facilitate Online Booking: Implementing an easy-to-use online booking system will streamline the reservation process, allowing customers to book their trips conveniently. This will also help us manage bookings more efficiently and reduce administrative overhead.

Audience

Our water rafting website is designed to cater to three primary target audiences: adventure enthusiasts, families, and corporate groups. Each segment has unique characteristics and needs that our website aims to address.

Who are the target customers?

  • Adventure Enthusiasts:
    • Age: Primarily 20-45 years old.
    • Financial Situation: Middle to upper-middle income, with disposable income for recreational activities and travel.
    • Interests: Outdoor adventures, water sports, travel, fitness, and nature. They are drawn to adrenaline-pumping activities and exploring new experiences.
    • Lifestyle: Active and health-conscious, often participating in hiking, camping, kayaking, and other outdoor activities.
  • Families:
    • Age: Parents aged 30-50, children aged 8-18.
    • Financial Situation: Stable income, budgeting for family vacations and activities.
    • Interests: Family bonding experiences, outdoor activities, educational and recreational trips. They seek safe, fun, and memorable experiences for the entire family.
    • Lifestyle: Family-oriented, prioritizing activities that can be enjoyed together, with an emphasis on safety and educational value.
  • Corporate Groups:
    • Age: Typically 25-55 years old.
    • Financial Situation: Businesses with budgets for employee engagement and team-building activities.
    • Interests: Team-building, corporate retreats, leadership development, and fostering teamwork.
    • Lifestyle: Professional, goal-oriented, seeking activities that enhance team dynamics and employee satisfaction.

What do they want?

  • Adventure Enthusiasts: Thrilling and challenging rafting experiences, opportunities to explore new locations, and activities that provide an adrenaline rush.
  • Families: Safe and enjoyable experiences for all family members, including age-appropriate activities, educational aspects, and opportunities for family bonding.
  • Corporate Groups: Team-building exercises, opportunities to foster teamwork and communication, and unique experiences that can be shared among colleagues.

Scenario Questions

Scenario Question: An adventure enthusiast in their early 30s, who frequently travels and participates in extreme sports, visits the website. What are they looking for?

Answer: They are seeking detailed information on the most challenging and unique rafting trips available, including difficulty levels, safety measures, guides' expertise, and real-time availability for easy booking. They also want to see high-quality images and videos showcasing the adventure and excitement of the trips.

Scenario Question: A family of four, with children aged 10 and 15, is planning their summer vacation. What specific information will they need from the website?

Answer: They need information on family-friendly rafting trips, including safety protocols, age recommendations, and activities suitable for children. They will look for testimonials from other families, detailed packing lists, and reassurances regarding the safety and enjoyment of the experience for all family members.

Scenario Question: A human resources manager is tasked with organizing a team-building event for their company. What details will they be searching for on the website?

Answer: They will search for corporate packages that include team-building exercises, information on how these activities can benefit team dynamics, and logistics details for planning the group trip. They will also look for case studies of previous corporate trips, detailed itineraries, and pricing options that align with the company's goals and budget.
